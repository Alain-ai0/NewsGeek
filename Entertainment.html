MAPPA Celebrates 15 Years: Studio Drops Massive Trailers for Upcoming Shonen Slate

To mark its 15th anniversary, animation powerhouse studio MAPPA held a landmark celebration showcasing their next generation of production plans. Kicking things off with immense momentum, the studio released the official teaser trailer for Jujutsu Kaisen Season 4, confirming that the highly anticipated adaptation of the grueling Culling Game arc is actively in development. Production shifts see Takeru Sato stepping into the main director's seat while Shota Goshozono elevates to Chief Director, promising an evolution in the series' definitive visual presentation.
