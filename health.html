Neural Stem Cell Frontiers: REGEN4HD Enters Phase 1b Human Testing Trials

In a historic step for regenerative neurology, clinical investigators have officially initiated the REGEN4HD trial protocol, dosing the first human participant with hNSC-01 neural stem cells. By physically anchoring immature cells directly into the striatum, medical teams aim to evaluate safety thresholds while assessing if these engineered cellular clusters can repair, stabilize, or completely rebuild deep neural pathways damaged by hereditary cognitive decline.
